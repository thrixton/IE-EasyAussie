Melbourne Living Area


Melbourne had been voted the world’s most livable city from 2011 to 2018. Even in 2019, Melbourne loses world’s most livable city, but it still is a very attractive city with fantastic quality of life. The local coffee, music, arts, sport, food and their lifestyle always share Melbourne’s passions with the people from all over the world. The lifestyle and culture will make you never want to leave this beautiful city.
